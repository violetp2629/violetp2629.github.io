Movie Webpage Project

During this project, I applied all my gathered knowledge of HTML and CSS into creating an original movie poster, called "The Remnant Operation".

The basic premise of the fake movie was a horror/thriller that revolved around human experimentation via augmented reality. A group of people from around the world are invited to partake in a scientific investigation, with the promise of all services paid in full. However, things don't go as planned. Knocked unconscious, the group awakes on a desolate tree-covered island, full of dangerous tests and obstacles that will surely isolate the strong from the weak. The catch? The island is surrounded by a lethal forcefield, which continues to shrink. Our main characters, Wilson and V, must find a way to escape before time runs out.

Over the course of our work, the most difficult aspects were plot brainstorming and design.
